Mobile App to sell NBA tickets and upgrades

UX System Design, Prototyping, Usability Tests, iA, and Visual

We user tested 2 visual concepts of our best hypothesis for an ideal user experience buying tickets and upgrades at an NBA game. This test (from April 2016) represents part of a much longer UX redesign process that we began in January 2016 at Experience (expapp.com). We invited real customers into our office to test prototypes (built in Axure) against the live-in-production design. This product sells tens of thousands of tickets via mobile on behalf of 100+ NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL and NCAA teams. We moderated tests with over 10 real Atlanta Hawks customers. Our tests relied on a ~new software called Lookback.io to run fully immersive usability tests that recorded both vocal and facial responses as well as every tap on the screen at the same time.
